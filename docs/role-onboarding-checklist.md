# Role Onboarding Checklist

> **Changelog:** Added in PR "docs: expand personas and add role matrix & checklists" (2026-04-23). Relates to #4.

## Purpose

This checklist helps a new team member onboard smoothly to a project. It covers access setup, key introductions, early deliverables, and ongoing rhythm. Adapt sections to match the specific role being onboarded (Developer, QA Lead, UX Designer, etc.).

For role descriptions and responsibilities, see [Roles and Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md).  
For a view of who owns what across activities, see the [Role Responsibility Matrix](./octoacme-role-responsibility-matrix.md).

---

## How to use this checklist

1. Copy this template into the project's documentation area or issue tracker.
2. Assign an **owner** to each item (suggested owners are noted in brackets).
3. Track completion during 1:1s or onboarding syncs.
4. Update the checklist for role-specific items before the new team member starts.

---

## Pre-Start (Before Day 1)

> Owner: **PM / PdM / Lead Developer** (coordinate with HR/IT as needed)

- [ ] Grant access to version control (GitHub org and relevant repositories) — *[PM / Lead Developer]*
- [ ] Provision credentials for required services (cloud console, CI/CD platform, monitoring tools) — *[DevOps Engineer / PM]*
- [ ] Add to communication channels (Slack/Teams workspace, project channels, mailing lists) — *[PM]*
- [ ] Add to calendar invites for standing meetings (standup, sprint planning, retrospective) — *[PM / Scrum Master]*
- [ ] Share links to key documents: project charter, roadmap, team working agreements, this onboarding checklist — *[PM / PdM]*
- [ ] Assign a **mentor** or onboarding buddy from the team — *[PM / Lead Developer]*
- [ ] Confirm role-specific tooling licenses are provisioned (e.g., design tool seat for UX Designer, test management tool for QA Lead) — *[PM / DevOps Engineer]*

---

## Week 1 — Orientation

> Owner: **Mentor / PM / PdM**

- [ ] Meet with Project Manager: understand project goals, timeline, and current status — *[PM]*
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager: understand product vision, roadmap, and open questions — *[PdM]*
- [ ] Meet with Lead Developer: walk through the codebase, architecture, and development workflow — *[Lead Developer]*
- [ ] Review key project artifacts:
  - [ ] Project charter / one-pager
  - [ ] Current sprint backlog and board
  - [ ] Architecture / design decision records (ADRs)
  - [ ] Role Responsibility Matrix ([octoacme-role-responsibility-matrix.md](./octoacme-role-responsibility-matrix.md))
- [ ] Attend at least one daily standup and observe the team's workflow — *[Mentor]*
- [ ] Review the team's Definition of Done and contribution guidelines — *[Lead Developer]*
- [ ] Understand escalation paths: who to contact for blockers, security issues, production incidents — *[PM / Lead Developer]*
- [ ] Complete any required compliance or security training — *[PM / HR]*

---

## Weeks 2–4 — Ramp-Up

> Owner: **Mentor / Lead Developer**

- [ ] Shadow relevant team members in key activities for the role:
  - Developer: pair on a small PR or bug fix — *[Lead Developer]*
  - QA Lead: observe a test planning session and defect triage — *[Lead Developer / QA Lead]*
  - UX Designer: attend a design review or user research session — *[Mentor]*
  - Data Analyst: review an existing dashboard and discuss metric definitions — *[PdM / Mentor]*
  - Scrum Master: co-facilitate a ceremony with the existing Scrum Master or PM — *[PM / Scrum Master]*
  - DevOps Engineer: walk through CI/CD pipeline and deployment runbook — *[Lead Developer / DevOps Engineer]*
- [ ] Complete first deliverable for the role (small in scope; agreed with mentor and PM) — *[Mentor]*
- [ ] Participate in a sprint review or demo — *[PM / Scrum Master]*
- [ ] Receive initial feedback from mentor on work quality and collaboration — *[Mentor]*
- [ ] Document any gaps in onboarding materials or processes and share with PM — *[New team member]*
- [ ] Confirm understanding of role responsibilities against the [Roles and Personas doc](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — *[Mentor]*

---

## Ongoing — Steady State

> Owner: **PM / Mentor / New team member**

- [ ] Establish regular 1:1 cadence with PM or direct manager — *[PM]*
- [ ] Confirm ownership of relevant metrics from the [Role Responsibility Matrix](./octoacme-role-responsibility-matrix.md) — *[PM / PdM]*
- [ ] Participate in team retrospectives and contribute to continuous improvement — *[Scrum Master / PM]*
- [ ] Keep personal documentation, dashboards, or runbooks up to date — *[New team member]*
- [ ] Attend relevant cross-functional syncs (e.g., design reviews, architecture discussions, stakeholder updates) — *[PM / Mentor]*
- [ ] Complete a 30/60/90-day check-in with PM to review progress and adjust goals — *[PM]*
